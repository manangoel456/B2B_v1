**Format:** YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reel / TikTok
**Duration:** 50 - 60 seconds
**Pacing:** Fast-paced, high energy, educational

### Tips for Editing & Production:
1. **The First 3 Seconds:** Ensure the visual hook is highly detailed and confusingly realistic. The contrast between "static image" and "cinematic video" is what keeps viewers from swiping away.
2. **On-Screen Text:** Use bold, high-contrast, centered captions (like yellow and white text) for every word spoken to maximize retention.
3. **Logo Placement:** Insert your brand logo in the top corner throughout the video, or use it as a full-screen transition overlay at **0:50** during the call to action.
